Meet Charles. Musician. Foodie. Web developer. Charles has a background in college courses focusing on web development and general programming at Coastal Carolina University. He founded Grey Man Web Designs in December of 2020 (quarantine, anyone?).

Charles is married with two children and currently resides in the Myrtle Beach area of coastal South Carolina. While focusing on small, locally owned businesses, he is continuously searching for other opportunities to push Grey Man Web Designs past the city limits of Myrtle Beach and eventually have clients across the country.
